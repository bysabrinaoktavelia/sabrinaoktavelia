## The Thesis

Most AI content in Southeast Asia sounds like it was written for Silicon Valley. The business owner in Surabaya who runs her garment workshop on WhatsApp has never been asked what she actually needs.

The content that changes behaviour is not the content that explains how AI works. It is the content that makes the audience feel capable of using it before they have even tried.
