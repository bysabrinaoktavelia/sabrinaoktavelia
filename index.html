About

Not one thing.
All the right things.

I am a bilingual creative generalist based in Jakarta — comfortable in a boardroom, on a podcast set, behind a makeup chair, or in front of a camera. My work sits at the intersection of communication, craft, and people.

I spent 16 months at Life Management Science Labs producing podcast content, running advisory programmes, and facilitating live sessions for international audiences — all while developing curriculum from academic research across 159 topic areas.

Currently building asahasa, a free English and employability education initiative for Indonesian youth, running Business English 101 and a mock interview programme. I believe clarity is a creative act — and that the most useful person in any room is the one who can move between strategy and execution without losing either.
